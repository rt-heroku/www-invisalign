Invisalign innovations.

Pushing what's possible.

We’ve been pushing the possibilities of the Invisalign System since 1997. We are the most advanced clear aligner system in the world, constantly imagining and developing new technologies to help people move forward with their lives. It’s how we’ve helped millions of people to find their best smile.

See your journey before it begins.

Become an Invisalign patient and preview your new smile before treatment even begins with our ClinCheck® technology that digitally maps your entire Invisalign journey. Your Invisalign Provider designs a precise, personalised treatment plan, including the exact movements of your teeth and how long your treatment will be.

Request an appointment with an Invisalign Provider

Movement that’s 75% more predictable.

When it comes to your smile, precision and predictability are crucial for a comfortable and efficient experience. Invisalign clear aligners are made of SmartTrack material which provides greater than 75% improvement in overall tooth movement predictability1.

Each aligner change is a small step towards your big transformation, moving your teeth comfortably and efficiently towards their final position. 

And if your Invisalign Provider advises that you are suitable to change your aligners every week rather than every two weeks, you could achieve your new smile in as little as 3 months, that’s up to 50% faster than before2.

Advancing digital dentistry.

We advanced digital dentistry with the introduction of iTero®. Replacing manual impressions, our intra-oral scanner creates fast, precise 3D digital images of your teeth that are used to develop your treatment plan4.

The future is being 3D printed.

We’re leading the digital transformation in dentistry and orthodontics, making metal and analogue a thing of the past as we push the boundaries forward.

We are the world’s largest user of state-of-the-art 3D printing technology for making highly accurate, customised aligners3.
