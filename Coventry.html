Align Technology makes no guarantee or assurance of achievability of any particular treatment outcome or that you will be an eligible candidate for Invisalign treatment. Only your Invisalign Provider can determine your suitability for Invisalign treatment and treatment options.

All Invisalign Providers listed in the "Find an Invisalign Provider" feature have completed the training course offered by Align Technology, necessary to enable him/her to begin treating patients with the Invisalign System. The Invisalign designations (e.g., Platinum Elite (PE), Platinum (P)) are primarily based on the relative number of historical Invisalign treatments an Invisalign Provider has submitted or has committed to submit. The Invisalign Provider status and the Invisalign designations are not recognized as specialty areas by the applicable dental associations within your country.

The order of appearance of an Invisalign Provider in the search results is primarily based on the historical number of Invisalign treatments an Invisalign Provider has submitted and has committed to submit. The Invisalign Provider list is not exhaustive, and there may be other Invisalign-trained providers in your area.
